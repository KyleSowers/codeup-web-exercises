“It’s not supposed to be easy to solve a global problem. You have to do whatever it takes.” Pioneering Solutions for a Zero-Emissions World.

Nikola recently attended the ACT Expo, where the most advanced fleet vehicles, fueling solutions and, technologies converged. With record-breaking attendance, Michael Lohscheller, Nikola’s CEO, delivered the opening keynote presentation and discussed the most abundant element in the world —hydrogen. Nikola’s presence was further amplified by up-close and personal hands-on experiences, including ride-and-drives for over 200 attendees in the Nikola Tre battery-electric and hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicles. Come along with us as we share this event celebrating advancements in clean technology.

Fueling forward to zero emissions for the future of our planet. #Hydrogen is the most abundant element in the universe, and the options to utilize this #energy source are limitless. We believe hydrogen is the answer to a sustainable future. We’re on a mission to use hydrogen for long-distance, heavy commercial transportation and to provide a complete solution that includes hydrogen fuel production, dispensing, and consumption for all. It’s a win-win for both the future of transportation and the world.
